Hey guys! This is a little snippet about myself and about Anilo. My name is Dallin and I'm a student currently living in Utah. I'm studying aerospace engineering and I also made this business because I absolutely love carbon fiber. I also really love the rings I make. I originally started making carbon fiber rings about a year ago with my brother. He also has a carbon fiber ring business and has been extremely successful and is actually a partner of Anilo Carbon. He showed me the ropes and helped me machine my first rings. Then it turned into a real job as I began making most of his customers orders.

I got really good at making them and I could produce them very quickly. I now take pride in every ring I make and quality is my goal.
